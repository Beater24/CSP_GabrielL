Why is LeBron better than Jordan?

Yes I do enjoy debating in favor about why LeBron is the GOAT, This is the only time I write an essay with PASSION. This is a two paragraph essay to prove on why LeBron's all around skills such as defending or scoring, his longetivity and his ability to adapt to the modern game, and his influence throughout not just the NBA but the entire sport of basketball, makes him the goat.

LeBron James is often considered the "GOAT" (Greatest Of All Time) due to his unparalleled longevity, all-around skills, and impact on the game, including his record-breaking statistics, multiple championships, and consistent dominance over two decades.

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the reasons why LeBron is frequently labeled as the GOAT:

Longevity and Consistency:

LeBron has maintained an incredibly high level of play for an unprecedented 22 seasons, a feat that is unmatched in NBA history.

All-Around Game:

He excels at scoring, passing, rebounding, and defending, making him a truly versatile player who can impact the game in multiple ways.

Record-Breaking Statistics:

LeBron holds numerous NBA records, including the most points scored in NBA history, and has consistently led the league in multiple statistical categories.

Championship Success:

He has won four NBA championships, four NBA Finals MVP awards, and four regular season MVP awards, demonstrating his ability to lead his teams to the top.

Adaptability:

LeBron has consistently adapted his game throughout his career, adding new skills and improving his existing ones, showcasing his ability to evolve with the game.

Impact on the Game:

LeBron's influence extends beyond his statistics, as he has inspired a generation of players and fans, and has been a leader both on and off the court.

Player Polls:

According to a player poll, LeBron is gaining ground on Michael Jordan in the GOAT debate, but Jordan is still ahead. But Jordan played in an era where the best players from the previous era retired which allowed Jordan to easily cruise through the league with six championships.

Here's why some people suggest LeBron ISN'T the GOAT

LeBron James is often debated as not being the "GOAT" (Greatest Of All Time) because of his lack of consistent dynasty-level success, even with multiple championships. While he's made it to 8 straight NBA Finals, he only won 2 of 4 with the Miami Heat and 1 of 4 with the Cleveland Cavaliers. This contrasts with players like Michael Jordan, who won 6 championships in a 6-year period, and Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, who had a dynasty with multiple championships in both Milwaukee and Los Angeles.
